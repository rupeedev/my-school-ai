What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. The term may also be applied to any machine that exhibits traits associated with a human mind such as learning and problem-solving.

Why is it Important?

AI is transforming every walk of life. From healthcare to finance, and from daily errands to space exploration, AI is everywhere.


  • Automation of repetitive tasks
  • Intelligent decision making
  • Solving complex problems
  • Creating personalized experiences

AI in Daily Life

You might be using AI without even knowing it! Here are some common examples:

  • Voice Assistants like Siri and Alexa
  • Recommendation systems in Netflix and YouTube
  • Face ID unlocking in smartphones
  • Self-driving features in modern cars

Common Questions (Interactive)

Will AI replace humans?

AI is designed to augment human capabilities, not replace them entirely. It handles repetitive tasks, allowing humans to focus on creativity.

Is AI safe?

AI safety is a major field of research. Responsible development and ethical guidelines are key to ensuring AI benefits humanity.
